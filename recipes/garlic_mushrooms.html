Garlic Mushrooms

What is Garlic Mushrooms

These garlic mushrooms are easy to make by saut√©ing sliced mushrooms in butter with red wine. Delicious with grilled steaks or on top of toasted bread for a tasty appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 pounds sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon gried basil
  • 1 cup red wine

Directions

Step 1
Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add mushrooms and garlic; cook and stir until mushrooms are a light golden brown and liquid has evaporated, about 10 minutes. Stir in basil.
Step 2
Reduce heat to low, and pour wine into the skillet. Simmer until wine has mostly evaporated. Serve immediately.
